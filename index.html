The Genesis

TYCASE was born from a simple frustration: why must we compromise between beautiful design and military-grade protection?

Each design is hand-illustrated by our in-house artists, then embedded into our proprietary shock-absorbing polymer using a sublimation process that ensures the artwork never fades, scratches, or peels.
